Project Title: Smart Energy Monitoring System

Objective:
To monitor electrical signal levels and detect high energy usage.

Components:
Arduino UNO
Potentiometer
LED
Resistor

Concept:
The Arduino reads analog voltage signals and converts them into digital values using ADC.

Working:
If the signal value exceeds a threshold level, the LED turns ON to indicate high energy usage.
